REFRESH art/tech

The art and technology community has historically amplified the worst aspects of both the tech world and the art world resulting in a system which undervalues the work of entire populations of creators, and compounds broader cultural oppressions including those directed at women, people of color, people with disabilities, and LGBTQ+ individuals.

Through their refusal to address systemic sexism and eurocentrism institutions like Ars Electronica reinforce this exclusionary status quo.

We say #KissMyArs

Coming in fall of 2017 we will showcase the work of artists from diverse backgrounds highlighting voices which run counter to the mainstream, with an aim to opening public dialogue around the problems, benefits, and tradeoffs new technology is bringing.

It’s time to REFRESH our ideas of what art and technology can and should be.
